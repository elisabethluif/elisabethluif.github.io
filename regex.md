# Regex practice

## PART I

1. What regular expression matches each of the following?
	“eat”, “eats”, “ate”, “eaten”, “eating”, “eater”,
	“eatery”

**Solution:**
`\b(eat(s|en|ing|ery?)?|ate)\b`
